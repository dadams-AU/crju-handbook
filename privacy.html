Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 11, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how the California State University, Fullerton (CSUF) CRJU Undergraduate Advising Handbook website handles information and data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We do not collect any personal information from visitors to this website. You can browse the entire CRJU Handbook without providing any personal data.

Server Logs

Like most websites, our server automatically collects limited technical information for security and maintenance purposes:

  • IP addresses (anonymized)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referring website (if any)
  • Pages accessed and timestamps

This information is used solely for website maintenance, security monitoring, and improving user experience. It is not shared with third parties and is retained only as long as necessary for these purposes.

Cookies and Tracking

This website does NOT use:

  • Cookies (first-party or third-party)
  • Tracking scripts
  • Analytics services (Google Analytics, etc.)
  • Advertising pixels
  • Social media tracking

FERPA Compliance

As part of California State University, Fullerton, this website complies with the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA). No student educational records or personally identifiable information from education records are collected, stored, or shared through this website.

Third-Party Links

This website contains links to external websites, including:

  • CSUF official websites (fullerton.edu domains)
  • University system resources (calstate.edu)
  • Government resources (.gov domains)
  • Academic and professional organizations

Important: We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of external websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any website you visit.

Security Measures

We implement reasonable security measures to protect this website and its users:

  • HTTPS encryption: All connections use secure HTTPS protocol
  • Regular updates: Website software and dependencies are kept current
  • Security monitoring: Server logs are monitored for suspicious activity
  • Access controls: Administrative access is restricted to authorized personnel
  • No data storage: Since we don't collect personal data, there is no personal information to breach

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we take reasonable measures to protect against unauthorized access.

Changes to This Policy

CSUF may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. Any changes will be:

  • Reflected on this page with an updated "Effective Date"
  • Communicated through the Criminal Justice department if changes materially affect your rights

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about our privacy practices.
